\begin{enumerate}
\item There is no consistent pattern of growth trends the temperature and water availability across years.
\item The highest growth year (2018) didn't happen in either the warmest or wettest year.
\item The lowest growth was during the driest, but also the warmest season. 
\item In terms of season length, we don't observe a clear trend either.
\item The highest growth in 2018 was neither the earliest or latest SOS and EOS or the longest season. 
\item The least growth happened in the shortest season, but during the latest SOS unlike what we expected (warmer conditions experienced when SOS is late would lead to increased growth). 
\end{enumerate}
